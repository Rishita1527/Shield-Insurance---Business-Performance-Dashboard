## üìÅ Project Overview

Shield Insurance is a customer-focused insurance provider operating across major Indian metro cities. The company offers diverse policy solutions catering to multiple age groups, focusing on accessibility, agent support, and digital growth through online platforms.

This analysis focuses on:

- **Overall business performance**
- **Revenue and customer trends**
- **City-wise insights**
- **Sales mode performance**
- **Age group behaviour**
- **Identifying growth opportunities & risks**

## üîç Key Insights

- **March 2023** was the strongest month for both revenue and customer acquisition.  
- **Delhi NCR + Mumbai** generated almost **65%** of total revenue.  
- The **31‚Äì50 age group** is the company‚Äôs core revenue segment.  
- **Digital channels contribute ~30%** and show strong stability.  
- **65+ customers** contribute high premiums but also higher settlement exposure.  
- **Indore** and the **18‚Äì24 age group** show long-term growth potential.

## üöÄ Business Recommendations

- Scale **digital channels** to support long-term growth.  
- Strengthen **offline agent network** in Tier-2 cities.  
- Prioritize marketing for the **31‚Äì50 age segment**.  
- Build entry-level digital products for the **18‚Äì30 youth segment**.  
- Improve risk management for **65+ high-value** customers.
